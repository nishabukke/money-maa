    What is PMS?

    Portfolio Management Services (PMS) is a tailored and professionally overseen investment avenue that employs diverse strategies to capitalize on market-linked opportunities. Tailored to suit the preferences and risk appetite of high-net-worth individuals (HNIs), PMS offers a direct investment approach into a diversified range of securities, including equities, fixed income, and structured products. This personalized service is designed to provide HNIs with exposure to the market while allowing for customization based on their financial goals and risk tolerance.

    Money Maa is aligned with a broad spectrum of esteemed third-party PMS providers throughout India. These providers, including asset management companies (AMCs) and financial institutions, bring forth specialized PMS offerings. By partnering with a diverse range of providers, DhruvNivesh aims to offer clients a comprehensive selection of PMS options, enabling them to choose strategies that align with their investment objectives and preferences.

    In essence, Portfolio Management Services through DhruvNivesh act as a gateway for high-net-worth individuals to access professionally managed investment portfolios that are tailored to their unique financial circumstances, risk tolerance, and market exposure preferences.

    Alternate Investment Funds

    Alternate Investment Funds (AIFs) encompass a diverse range of investment options, including real estate, private equity, hedge funds, venture capital funds, and unconventional stock and bond strategies such as long/short or arbitrage. These alternatives deviate from traditional investment approaches, offering investors the potential for unique risk-return profiles.

    Given that alternative investments often exhibit distinct behaviors compared to conventional stocks and bonds, their inclusion in a portfolio can contribute to broader diversification, risk reduction, and potential returns enhancement. DhruvNivesh has established partnerships with distinguished third-party AIF providers, positioning itself to offer investors access to a spectrum of high-return opportunities over the long term.

    By collaborating with reputable AIF providers, DhruvNivesh facilitates investors in diversifying their portfolios with unconventional and potentially lucrative investment avenues. The aim is to provide clients with access to a variety of alternative investment strategies, allowing them to tailor their portfolios to align with their risk preferences, financial goals, and the pursuit of long-term returns.
